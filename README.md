# 📊 Regional Sales Analysis & Business Intelligence Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project performs an end-to-end analysis of regional sales data to uncover business insights and build an interactive dashboard for decision-making.

The analysis focuses on identifying revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and regional sales patterns using historical data.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations face challenges in:

* Inconsistent revenue and profit across regions
* Lack of visibility into seasonal trends
* Difficulty in identifying top-performing products and customers
* Limited understanding of channel-wise performance

👉 Goal: Use data analytics to identify growth opportunities and optimize business strategy. 

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 📅 Sales Trends

* Strong seasonality observed
* Peak: May–June
* Lowest: January

### 🏆 Product Performance

* Products 25 & 26 contribute ~25% of total revenue
* Low-performing products identified for optimization

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

* West region dominates sales
* Northeast underperforms → growth opportunity

### 🏪 Channel Analysis

* Wholesale contributes ~54% of total sales
* Export provides higher profit margins

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Revenue is concentrated among top customers
* High-value customers show strong profitability patterns

### 💰 Profit Drivers

* Unit price is the strongest driver of revenue and profit
* Quantity has relatively low impact

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard provides:

* Sales trend analysis over time
* Product and category performance
* Regional and state-level insights
* Customer segmentation analysis
* Interactive filtering (time, region, product, channel)

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 🎯 Launch seasonal campaigns to stabilize revenue
* 🛍 Focus on high-performing products (25 & 26)
* 🌍 Increase investment in low-performing regions
* 🚀 Expand export channels for higher margins
* 📉 Monitor low-margin transactions and optimize pricing
